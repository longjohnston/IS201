My Connection to Baseball

I grew up in Los Angeles and have always been a fan of the Dodgers. Some of my earliest memories are going to games with my dad and watching them together at home. Those experiences made baseball something I’ve always enjoyed and followed closely.

I also played baseball throughout my childhood and into high school, which helped me appreciate the game at a deeper level.

  1. Why baseball is important to me:
    • Spending time with my dad growing up
    • Competing and improving as a player
    • Following the Dodgers each season
  2. How I stay involved today:
    • Watching Dodgers games regularly
    • Participating in fantasy baseball leagues
    • Analyzing player performance and matchups
